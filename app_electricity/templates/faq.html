Frequently Asked Questions

You can pay your electricity bill online by logging into your account, selecting the 'Pay Bill' option, and following the on-screen instructions.

We accept various payment methods, including credit/debit cards, net banking, and UPI.

You can view your payment history by navigating to the 'Recent Payments' section in your account dashboard.

If your payment fails, please try again after a few minutes. If the issue persists, contact customer support for assistance.
